The Five V's:Value

The final, and arguably the most important V to define Big Data is value.

Deriving insight from data isn't all that constitutes value. You must also look at the cost and benefit of obtaining and analysing such data. What social value does it bring? What monetary value does it bring? What are the ethical costs?

Re-examine the examples in the first section and describe the value created by each data source? (Compare the examples to an alternative that does not utilise Big Data. For example Spotify vs A User Curated Playlist. Google Maps vs a traditional directory). Consider the value for both consumers, companies and society as a whole.
