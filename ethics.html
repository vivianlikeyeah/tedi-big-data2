Ethics

Big Data allows us to do things we never knew to be possible before. This new realm brings with it a new set of ethical concerns, that evolve alongside the Big Data movement. Below we explore both the current and future ethical concerns of Big Data.

However, science often follows science-fiction. Do you think it is currently possible to implement these systems, given what you have learnt about Big Data? How would you do so, given today's technology?

It may not be an implantable chip in your brain, but it may very well be a VR headset or some form of wearble technology?
