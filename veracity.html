The Five V's:Veracity

Veracity refers to the quality of the data as well as the trustworthiness of the data source, type and processing. This involves separating 'data' from 'noise', removing bias, abnormalities, duplication etc. It also involves interpreting the data correctly.

Factors that impact the veracity of data

There are several factors that contribute to the veracity of data
including Bias, Software Bugs, Falsification, Human Error, Precision etc.
